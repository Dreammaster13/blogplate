ASTRONAUT

Astronauts have to be young and not older than 40 years old and height can not be more than 5'11. They should also have 1,500 hours of flight time to qualify. Cosmonauts on the other hand should be young and not older than 30 years of age with a maximum height of 5'7. They need 900 hours of flight time to qualify.
